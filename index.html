Privacy Policy — AI Tycoon

Effective date: July 2, 2026 · Developer: Paweł Kusiak · Contact: pawelkusiak92@gmail.com

AI Tycoon (“the app”, “the game”) is a satirical idle game for iOS. This policy explains what data the game handles, what stays on your device, and what is shared with third parties — and why.

1. Data that stays on your device

  • Game progress (balances, businesses, upgrades, prestige, quest progress) is stored locally on your device. It is never uploaded to us — we do not operate any servers.
  • Settings (sound, music, appearance, haptics) are stored locally.
  • Local gameplay statistics (session length, screens visited, action counts) are computed and stored only on your device to help balance the game. They are never transmitted anywhere.

Deleting the app permanently removes all of the above.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The game shows optional rewarded ads served by Google AdMob. When ads are served, Google may collect and process data such as:

  • your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) — only if you allowed tracking in the App Tracking Transparency prompt,
  • IP address and coarse location derived from it,
  • device information and ad interaction data (views, taps).

If you allow tracking, ads may be personalized. If you choose “Ask App Not to Track”, Google serves non-personalized ads instead — the game works exactly the same. You can change your choice at any time in iOS Settings ▸ Privacy & Security ▸ Tracking.

How Google uses this data is described in How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services and the Google Privacy Policy.

For users in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom and Switzerland, a consent dialog compliant with the GDPR is shown before personalized ads are served, and your choices can be updated from within the game.

3. In-app purchases

Purchases (the premium “Chips” currency and offer packs) are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see or store your payment details. Apple's handling of this data is described in the Apple Privacy Policy.

4. Game Center

If you are signed in to Apple Game Center, the game submits your scores (lifetime earnings, prestige count) to Game Center leaderboards under your Game Center player profile. This is handled by Apple according to the Apple Privacy Policy. You can disable Game Center for this game in iOS Settings.

5. Notifications

With your permission, the game schedules local notifications (e.g. “your businesses have been idle”). These are generated on your device; no push servers are involved.

6. Children

The game is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children; the only third-party data processing (advertising) is described in section 2.

7. Your rights

Because we do not collect or store your personal data on any server, requests to access or delete data we hold will generally have nothing to act on — your data lives in the app on your device. For data processed by Google (advertising), please use Google's My Ad Center or the contact options in Google's privacy policy. You can also reset your device's advertising identifier in iOS settings at any time.

8. Changes to this policy

If the way the game handles data changes (for example, new ad networks or online features), this page will be updated and the effective date above revised.
